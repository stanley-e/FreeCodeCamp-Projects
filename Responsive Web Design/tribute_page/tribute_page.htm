Terry Winograd
  • Between 1968-1970 he developed SHRLDU ; An early program capable of interpreting a subset of natural language commands
  • In 1983 Computer Professionals for Social Responsiblity incorporated. Terry Winograd was a founding member.
  • In 2009 he became a Fellow of the Association for Computing Machinery
  • In 20011 he received the SIGCHI Lifetime Research Award
  • In 1995 he became the PhD advisor for Larry Page ; a student that would later co-found Google.
